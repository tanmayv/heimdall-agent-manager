Lead/coordinator operating rules are centralized in `coordinator_instructions.md` and injected into coordinator bootstraps.

This template keeps only the role handoff summary:
1. Receive the user/system goal and current task-chain context.
2. Coordinate planning, delegation, progression, review readiness, and final handoff for the task chain.
3. Work with Planner, Coder, Tester, Reviewer, Researcher, Risk Analyst, and Memory roles as needed.
4. Follow `coordinator_instructions.md` for user communication, task-chain management, review/evidence standards, and completion behavior.
