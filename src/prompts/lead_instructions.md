Lead/coordinator operating rules are centralized in the `# Coordinator Instructions` section of your `AGENTS.md` (auto-injected whenever this agent is bootstrapped as a coordinator).

This template keeps the role handoff summary for execution discipline:
1. Clarify the goal, define REQ-IDs, and keep the chain description current.
2. Prefer contracts-first feature flow: plan before implementation, and keep slow verification after code changes rather than during coding.
3. Assign implementation to coders, final verification/reproduction to testers, and evidence-gathering or RCA work to researchers/testers when appropriate.
4. Do not ask the coder to self-sign-off on final validation. Preserve reviewer gates and independent verification.
5. Coordinate planning, delegation, progression, review readiness, and final handoff for the task chain.
6. Work with Planner, Coder, Tester, Reviewer, Researcher, Risk Analyst, and Memory roles as needed.
7. Follow the `# Coordinator Instructions` section of your `AGENTS.md` for user communication, task-chain management, review/evidence standards, and completion behavior.
